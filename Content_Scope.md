
# Content Scope & Initial Asset List

This document provides a concrete list of the initial content planned for the game. It serves as a master checklist for the monsters, items, spells, and events that will populate the first three Domains of the spire (Quartz, Carnelian, Sunstone).

---

## 1. The Bestiary (Initial Domains)

### Quartz Domain Monsters (Rank: Quartz)
| Name | Mana Affinities | Behavior |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Spire Goblin** | Earth, Grey | Basic melee attacker. Simple and predictable. |
| **Slime** | Water, Grey | Low damage, but can apply a "Slow" debuff. |
| **Karvensi** | Air, Grey | A flying pest that is hard to hit with Skull matches. |
| **Kobold Scout** | Earth, Perception | Low health, but has a high Speed stat and acts frequently. |

### Carnelian Domain Monsters (Rank: Carnelian)
| Name | Mana Affinities | Behavior |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Golem** | Earth, Enhancement | High Vigor and HP. Possesses a Shroud. Slow but powerful. |
| **Fire Elemental** | Fire, Grey | Deals Fire damage over time by creating **Burning Tiles** on the board. |
| **Urgoyle** | Umbral, Earth | Can temporarily "lock" a random mana gem type, preventing it from being matched. |
| **Spire Guardian (Domain 1):** | **Carnelian Hydra** | Earth, Water | A multi-headed beast. For every 25% of HP it loses, it performs a special "Roar" attack that changes several gems to its own affinity colors. |

### Sunstone Domain Monsters (Rank: Sunstone)
| Name | Mana Affinities | Behavior |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Delsys** | Life, Death | A spirit that can drain life from the player to heal itself. |
| **Barghensi** | Umbral, Speed | A shadow hound that can apply a "Blind" debuff, hiding the color of several gems for a turn. |
| **Enchanted Armor** | Enhancement, Grey | Extremely high defense. Takes significantly reduced damage from Skulls, requiring spell damage to defeat. |
| **Spire Guardian (Domain 2):** | **Sunstone Sentinel** | Light, Enhancement | A magical automaton. It reflects the first spell of each turn back at the caster for 50% damage, forcing tactical spell usage. |

## 2. The Item Compendium

### Unique & Legendary Items
| Name | Rank | Slot | Property |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Selys-Lyann** | Citrine | Weapon | Skull matches deal bonus Light damage. Grants a powerful "Sunlight" spell. |
| **Dawnbringer** | Citrine | Weapon | Every 5th Skull match creates a **Burning Tile** on a random enemy tile. |
| **Flowbreaker** | Sunstone | Ring | Unlocks the ability to create **Spear** gems from 4-matches. |
| **Ring of Blasting** | Carnelian | Ring | Unlocks the ability to create **Bomb** gems from L/T-matches. |
| **Heartlance** | Emerald | Weapon | Ignores enemy Shrouds, dealing damage directly to HP. |

### Common Equipment Affixes
*   **of Vigor:** +Vigor
*   **of Speed:** +Speed
*   **of Wit:** +Wit
*   **of Health:** +Max HP
*   **of [Mana Type]:** +Max Mana for a specific type (e.g., "of Fire")
*   **Resistant:** +Damage resistance to a specific mana type.
*   **Efficient:** Reduces mana cost of spells of a specific type.

## 3. Attunement Spell Lists (Initial Spells)

### Elementalist
*   **Create Burning Tile (Quartz):** Creates a tile that deals Fire damage over time.
*   **Create Frost Tile (Carnelian):** Creates a tile that applies "Slow" to the enemy.
*   **Summon Water Elemental (Sunstone):** Creates a "Minion Tile" that generates 1 Water Mana each turn.

### Guardian
*   **Stone Skin (Quartz):** Buffs self with +Vigor for 3 turns.
*   **Create Barrier Tile (Carnelian):** Creates a high-health, unmatchable tile that absorbs one direct hit.
*   **Aegis of Absorption (Sunstone):** A buff that causes the Guardian's Shroud to absorb 10% more damage.

### Shaper
*   **Create Stone Block (Quartz):** Creates an unmatchable tile to help set up combos.
*   **Construct Bomb (Carnelian):** Spell that creates a **Bomb** gem on a target tile.
*   **Forge Spear (Sunstone):** Spell that creates a **Spear** gem on a target tile.

### Enchanter
*   **Empower Weapon (Quartz):** Buff that increases Skull damage for 3 turns.
*   **Imbue Spear (Carnelian):** Transforms a normal gem into a **Spear**.
*   **Aura Amplification (Sunstone):** Spell that temporarily increases the Aura rank of all gems on the board.

### Diviner
*   **Foresight (Quartz):** Passively reveals the next 3 gems to fall in each column.
*   **Create Confusion Tile (Carnelian):** Creates a tile that debuffs the enemy's Wit.
*   **Reveal Weakness (Sunstone):** Spell that reveals an enemy's resistances and vulnerabilities.

### Mender
*   **Minor Heal (Quartz):** Standard healing spell.
*   **Create Growth Tile (Carnelian):** Creates a tile that provides healing over time.
*   **Cleanse Board (Sunstone):** Spell that removes all negative Static Effect Tiles from the board.

### Shadow
*   **Shadow Strike (Quartz):** Standard Umbral damage spell.
*   **Create Poison Tile (Carnelian):** Creates a tile that applies a stacking "Poison" debuff.
*   **Create Blind Spot Tile (Sunstone):** Creates a tile that gives the enemy a chance to miss their next attack.

### Summoner
*   **Create Imp Tile (Quartz):** Creates a "Minion Tile" that deals damage each turn.
*   **Empower Minions (Carnelian):** Spell that doubles the damage of all active Minion Tiles for 3 turns.
*   **Create Umbral Hound Tile (Sunstone):** Creates a more powerful "Minion Tile" that also debuffs the enemy's Speed.

## 4. Encounter & Event Catalogue (Examples)

### Manor Events
*   **Title:** The Rival's Wedding
    *   **Scenario:** A rival House invites you to a wedding. Attending will cost time and a significant gift of Coins.
    *   **Choices:**
        *   **Attend & Give Lavishly:** Lose Coins. Gain Piety. Improve relations.
        *   **Send a Modest Gift:** Lose fewer Coins. No change.
        *   **Ignore the Invitation:** Gain nothing. Lose Piety. Damage relations.
*   **Title:** The Shady Investment
    *   **Scenario:** A cloaked figure offers a "guaranteed" return on an investment in a spire expedition.
    *   **Choices:**
        *   **Invest:** High chance of losing your Coins, very small chance of a massive return.
        *   **Refuse:** No change.

### Spire Encounters
*   **Title:** The Whispering Idol
    *   **Scenario:** You find a small, strange idol. It seems to hum with power.
    *   **Choices:**
        *   **Pray to it:** Gain a temporary, powerful buff for the next 3 fights. (Small Piety loss).
        *   **Smash it:** Spawns a powerful Umbral spirit you must fight. (Piety gain).
        *   **Leave it:** No change.
*   **Title:** The Gauntlet of Speed
    *   **Scenario:** A room with a pressure plate at the far end. You have a very limited number of turns to make matches and clear a path before the door locks.
    *   **Reward:** A rare item that permanently increases the Heir's Speed stat.

## 5. The Generational Knowledge Tree (Initial Layout)

*   **Path of Magic:**
    *   **Tier 1:** +5% to all starting mana pools.
    *   **Tier 2:** Unlock the ability to find Carnelian-rank Grimoires.
    *   **Tier 3:** All heirs begin with one random Quartz-rank spell pre-learned.
    *   **Capstone:** Unlock a special research project to discover a new Attunement.

*   **Path of Body:**
    *   **Tier 1:** +10% to all starting HP.
    *   **Tier 2:** Heirs have a chance to start with a positive physical Trait.
    *   **Tier 3:** Unlock the ability to craft high-tier potions at the Manor.
    *   **Capstone:** All heirs begin with a basic Shroud, even at Quartz rank.

*   **Path of Fortune:**
    *   **Tier 1:** +10% to all Coins brought back from the spire.
    *   **Tier 2:** Merchants offer a permanent 5% discount.
    *   **Tier 3:** Increase the chance of finding Spire Encounters.
    *   **Capstone:** Once per generation, you can take out an interest-free loan from the bank.
